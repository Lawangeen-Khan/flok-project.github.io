Inborn error of protein metabolism (IEPM) is a shared journey - we connect at the intersection of life and science. Here, we lean on each other for daily help managing an IEPM. Here, we harness our collective data to drive patient-led research that will improve quality of life for the IEPM community. Here, we fly further together than we can alone. Welcome to the flok!

What is flok?

We support connection within the IEPM community, daily management of any IEPM, and patient-led advancements in care and treatment.

Welcome to flok, where patients managing inborn errors of protein metabolism (IEPM) drive advancements in care and treatment. With flok, patients and caregivers find support in managing an IEPM with community events and digital tools. We are a connection point for disorder management, community, research, and key partners such as biotech/pharma and food manufacturing. Together we inspire patient-led research to enhance quality of life and advance our shared future.

The flok app

Tools for everyday IEPM management, opportunities for scientific advancement

Access handy tools for daily management of an IEPM such as diet trackers and disorder-specific recipes. Connect to the IEPM community through wellness challenges. If you choose to participate, contribute your data to unlock care solutions that have not yet been medically or technically possible.
